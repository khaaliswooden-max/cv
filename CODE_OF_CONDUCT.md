# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This repository is a professional space. Everyone who interacts with it —
through issues, pull requests, or discussions — is expected to help keep it
welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free, regardless of background,
identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in all communication.
- Offer constructive, actionable feedback.
- Assume good faith and focus on the content, not the person.
- Respect the personal and biographical nature of the material here.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Using the contact details or personal data in this repository for
  unsolicited marketing, spam, or automated scraping.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all repository spaces (issues, pull
requests, discussions, and commit history) and in any public space when an
individual is representing the repository or its owner.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported by contacting the repository owner through the channels listed in
the [README](./README.md#-contact). All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.

The repository owner is responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable
behavior and will take appropriate corrective action in response to any
behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, or harmful, including editing or
removing comments, commits, issues, and pull requests, or blocking
contributors.
