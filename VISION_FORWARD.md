## 🚀 Strategic Vision

My vision is to architect planetary-scale systems that fundamentally transform how organizations navigate complexity—from regulatory compliance to technological governance to procurement intelligence. I'm building the infrastructure layer for AI-augmented decision-making in high-stakes domains where correctness, security, and verifiability are non-negotiable.

**Core Thesis:** The convergence of AI, cryptography, and distributed systems enables self-improving platforms that learn from outcomes, prove compliance without exposing sensitive data, and scale from individual entities to global regulatory frameworks.
